Mom's Chicken Adobo

Total Time:30 mins


    Ingredients

  • 4 skin-on bone in chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup regular soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

    Instructions

  1. In a 8-inch pan or medium size dutch oven on medium heat, add oil.
    Once oil is hot place chicken thighs skin side down. Allow the skin to brown.
    Do not move the chicken after it hits the pan, this will prevent your chicken from properly browning.
  2. Pour in soy sauce, garlic, black peppercorns, bay leaves and lastly white vinegar.
  3. Bring to a boil. Then cover and reduce low-medium heat or a rolling simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Uncover the lid and flip the chicken over so the skin is facing up. Cover and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Uncover the lid and continue to simmer for 3-5 minutes so the sauce can thicken.
  6. Remove off the heat to serve & enjoy!
